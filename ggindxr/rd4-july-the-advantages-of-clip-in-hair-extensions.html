The Advantages of Clip in Hair Extensions

Clip in hair extensions are an affordable, easy to keep, low-maintenance way to adding length and volume to your daily appearance. Clip in hair extensions can be used to add hair anywhere on your head which makes them a great choice for adding length and fullness exactly where you need it.

Real hair extensions offer numerous benefits over synthetic clip-ins. They are more durable, last longer and are usually much more lightweight. When purchasing real hair extensions, you can be confident they will match your natural hair perfectly and will not tangle or fly away as they have been treated to maintain their appearance. Real hair extensions can be styled the same way as your own hair and they are generally made of high quality hair that has not been processed with hair dyes and bleach before being collected. Weft, or hair fiber made from virgin hair is used in the processing of real hair extensions so they have a shinier, smoother feel.

While they offer a more permanent solution, clip-in hair extensions only last between six and eight months before they need to be replaced. You can purchase many different colors and textures and lengths to match your natural hair or to create a sleek straight look, or a curlier style. When your hair extensions begin to show signs of wear and tear it is best to replace them. If you do care for and use them properly, they can last up to nine months.

Real hair extensions that are semi-permanently applied using glue are very durable and differ from clip ins in that they are not taken out on a daily basis. They can be styled just like your own hair and they are often made of high quality hair fibre, but they do not offer the versatility and practicality of clip ins. They are also usually much more costly as you will more than likely need to have them applied by a professional rather than applying them yourself at home.

If you are considering purchasing clip-ins for styling, you will want to look at the cost versus the results. Real hair products will last longer and provide better overall results. However, remy hair extensions can be expensive and only last between six months and 9 months to a year. Clip-ins can be styled as you would your natural hair and give you a lot of creative freedom when it comes to creating looks. Both synthetic hair and remy hair clip-ins are great solutions and should be considered for your regular hair product styling needs. If you choose synthetic hair extensions then be aware that many cannot withstand high heat and can melt when heat styled.

With clip-in hair extensions you can easily change your current style without the hassle of getting your natural hair cut by a professional stylist. Clip-ins come in many different lengths, styles and colors. You can get very short weaves or longer wefts depending on what you need. You can also get different kinds of extensions such as wefts, wavy, flat and curly. Clip ins are the easiest to style because all you have to do is attach them to your existing hair using the attached clips.

Wavy, flat and curly weaves are the most common wefts you see. These extensions work great for most hair types. However, curly wefts are more difficult to apply and will require you to curl your natural hair or wear your natural curl pattern if you have one. If you choose the right level of curliness of waviness you can easily apply a curly weave without a problem. If you need assistance, you can find a local stylist who specialises in hair extensions for styling guidance.

Remy hair clip in extensions offer many advantages over other popular methods of adding extra hair to your hair such as glued in hair extensions and synthetic weaves. The main advantage of clip in hair extensions is that they are natural looking and undetectable. This makes them great for women with very fine or thinning hair. Another advantage they have over glued in hair extensions is that they are quick and easy to apply. If you are not experienced with real hair, you will want to start out with clip in hair extensions because they are easier to work with.
