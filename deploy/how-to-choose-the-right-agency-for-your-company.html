How To Choose the Right Agency for Your Company

There are a huge number of ways that you can market your business online, such as social media, pay per click marketing, search engine optimisation, email marketing, and content marketing to name just a few. Agencies which specialise in marketing can simplify your commercial efforts, streamlining them and making them much more effective. This frees you up to do what you do best, and removes the stress of trying to handle marketing by yourself.

There are hundreds of agencies to choose from in the UK and each has its own special area of expertise. This can make it challenging to find the right agency that will help to realise your brand potential online, and form a unique and effective marketing strategy. Read on for our best advice at choosing the perfect agency for your needs.

Why choose an agency

If you run a Magento website you should look into taking on the services of a Magento 2 agency as they will have the most experience with your platform and will be able to market it more effectively. An agency will get to know the unique aspects of your business which will help to inform the direction they take your plan.

For example if you are marketing towards business Executives you should research channels that business executives tend to use in order to connect with brands online. If you are marketing towards a younger audience you can make use of channels such as Snapchat and Instagram as part of your marketing mix. An experienced Agency will understand the requirements of your target audience and will be able to market to them most effectively.

What will a marketing agency do for your business

External marketing agencies are tasked with getting to know your brand and helping you to identify your target audience, as this is the best way to focus your efforts. A digital agency will carry out in-depth market research on your behalf in order to gain a valuable and in-depth understanding of the kinds of people who are most likely to engage with your brand and consume your products.

Unfortunately there is never a one size fits all approach when it comes to marketing, making it necessary for professionals to create bespoke plans in order for them to be completely cost-effective and efficient.

Which techniques can be used to market a website

Social media is one of the most important marketing tools in existence today. Many people prefer to engage with brands through their social media channels, rather than visiting their websites or visiting their stores in person. Social media is considered one of the most personable and engaging channels in the marketing mix and some brands even choose to do all of their commercial operations via social media.

Search engine optimisation, also called SEO, has long been considered one of the most sustainable ways to market a website. It aims to build a strong brand reputation online whilst raising a company's profile in search engines. The higher a website appears in search engine results pages the more likely it will be to earn those all important clicks. Clicks convert to revenue when a Magento website meets a user's expectations and so SEO should form a part of any company's marketing strategy.

Paid advertising such as pay per click marketing and Google Ads are also popular ways to drive traffic to your website. By bidding on ad space online your brand will be able to compete with its major competitors, and by outbidding them they can even steal their site visitors and outcompete them.
